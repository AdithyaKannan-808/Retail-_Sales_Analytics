##  **Dashboard Objectives**

- Convert large transactional data into an **interactive decision-making tool**  
- Track key KPIs: **Sales, Profit, Orders, Return Rate, Segment Margin, Region Margin, Top Performer**  
- Identify **top-performing products, salespersons, regions, and buyer segments**  
- Reveal **profitability patterns** across time, categories, and customer types  
- Enable management to apply **dynamic slicers** for real-time insights

- **Total Sales**
- **Total Profit**
- **Total Orders**
- **Return Rate (%)**
- **Avg Profit per Order**
- **Buyer Segment Margin**
- **Region Margin**
- **Top Seller / Category**

---

##  **Key Insights**

- Technology & Office Supplies generated the highest profit margins (17%+).  
- Furniture category underperformed with lower profitability (~2.5%).  
- West region delivered the strongest profit margin (~21.9%), while Central underperformed.  
- Anna Andreadi was the most profitable salesperson.  
- Average return rate was ~8%, indicating moderate dissatisfaction or product mismatch.  
- Home Office customer segment showed strong margin stability (~14%).
